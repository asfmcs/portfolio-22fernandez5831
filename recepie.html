Chocolate Mug Cake

Time: 7 min
Serves:2

Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity Comments
flour 1/4 cup (self-rising)
Natural Strevia 1/4 cup recommended: SPLENDA® Naturals Stevia
cocoa 2 tbsp N/A
baking powder 1/3 tsp N/A
salt 1/8 tsp N/A
milk 4.5 tbsp N/A
coconut oil 1 tbsp N/A
water 3 tbsp N/A
vanilla 1/4 tsp N/A
chocolate chips 2 tsp mini size
strawberries 2 other fruit of choice can be added

Steps

  1. Begin by combining all your dry ingredients and pouring into a microwave-safe mug.

  2. Add wet ingredients and mix until well combined. Pour in 1 teaspoon of mini chocolate chips.

  3. Microwave on HIGH for 1 minute and 45 seconds. Top with remaining mini chips and strawberries. ENJOY!



Honey Mug Cake

Time: 5 min
Serves:2

Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity Comments
butter 2 tbsp do not overheat
runny honey 2 tbsp N/A
egg 1 (medium size)
vanilla 1/4 tbsp N/A
light brown sugar 3 tbsp N/A
self-ring flour 4 tbsp If you do not have self-raising flour,
substitute with plain (all-purpose)
flour and add ¼ tsp baking powder.

Steps

  1. Add the butter to a mug and melt in the microwave for around 10-20 seconds.
  2. Add the honey, egg and vanilla and beat with a fork until combined.
  3. Add the sugar, flour and salt and beat again until fully combined and smooth.
  4. Cook in the microwave for around 1 minute 15 seconds to 1 minute 30 seconds (depending on your microwave power), then allow to cool to room temperature.
  5. Meanwhile, to make the frosting, add the butter and icing (confectioners') sugar to another mug and use a fork to cut the butter into the icing sugar until it reaches a clumpy texture, then beat with the fork until fluffy, around 1-2 minutes).
  6. Spoon the frosting into a small piping bag or a ziplock bag fitted with an open star or plain round tip and pipe a swirl onto the top of the cooled cake.
  7. (Optional) Drizzle with extra honey and serve immediately.

Banana Mug Cake

Time: 3 min
Serves:1

Ingredients - batter

Ingredients Quantity Comments
flour 3 tbsp (self-rising)
brown sugar 3 tbsp N/A
baking powder 1/2 tsp dash of salt
butter 1 tbs melted
egg 1 lightly beaten
milk 1 tbs N/A
bannana 1 mashed

Ingredients - glaze

Ingredients Quantity Comments
flour 3 tbsp (self-rising)
brown sugar 1/4 cup N/A
butter 1 1/2 tbs N/A
vanilla 1/4 tsp small splash of milk

Steps

  1. In bowl, or mug itself mix together your flour, sugar and baking powder and salt.
  2. Stir in your butter, milk, banana, egg and vanilla until combined.
  3. Microwave for 3 minutes, checking halfway through.
  4. Let sit to cool.
